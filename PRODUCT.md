# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

AI operators, vibecoders, and daily AI power users who build, run, and coordinate AI agents, conversations, tasks, and customer workflows. They care about speed, control, privacy, and owning infrastructure instead of sending sensitive data through opaque vendor systems.

## Product Purpose

Zetta CRM is a self-hosted CRM for AI operators. It helps teams manage agents, accounts, conversations, tasks, workflows, and operator context in one private workspace. Success means visitors quickly understand: Zetta is fast to deploy, technically credible, bold in stance, and data-safe by default.

## Brand Personality

Fast, trusted, technical, data-safe, bold. Voice is short, declarative, confident, and ownership-obsessed. Copy should prove control and privacy without fear-mongering.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS cream pages, fake AI gradients, hype-heavy automation copy, finance-style navy/gold trust clichés, crypto-neon chaos, and decorative glassmorphism everywhere. Avoid designs that hide self-hosting behind vague platform language.

## Design Principles

- Ownership first: data, deployment, and operations belong to user.
- Proof over hype: technical claims must feel concrete.
- Fast comprehension: each section should explain one idea quickly.
- Privacy as architecture: safety is not badge, it is product stance.
- Bold but controlled: luminous anime-tech style, restrained enough to feel trustworthy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast. Respect reduced motion. Maintain keyboard focus indicators. Preserve readable typography on mobile. Avoid relying on color alone for meaning.
